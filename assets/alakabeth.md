# Alakabeth
*Female Cosmopolitan Dwarf Life Priest, Shrine-keeper*

## Lineage, Culture, and Background Features
ASI: +1 CON, +1 WIS
Speed: 30 ft
Features:
- Darkvision 60 ft
- Stone's Endurance: When you expend a hit die as part of a short rest, you can choose to either remove a level of exhaustion (keeping the rolled healing) or maximize the healing received from that hit die. You can use this feature once per short rest.
- Poison Resistance: Resist poison damage and adv. on saves against poison
- Coin-counter: Gain Haggler skill trick
- Babyl Dweller: You know the *lesser gift of tongues* incantation and can perform it without a ritual scroll and without expending the component.
Languages: Common, Yonwach, Lucian
Proficiencies: Religion, Persuasion, Insight, Wood-carver's tools, Hand drum, dice set
Equipment: A set of vestments, a holy symbol, and a pouch containing 10 gp
Skill Tricks: 
- Scholar: Religion
- Haggler

### Personality
- Devout, not forward or forceful
- Ideal is faith
- Bond is the sisterhood of Melara and the church tha traised her.
- Flaw: hesitates to engage, prefers to stand back
- Call to Adventure: Dream that called her to go fight undead and evil.
