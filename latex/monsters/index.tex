\section{Monsters by Challenge Rating}
This index presents links to monsters within bands of average challenge rating (calculated as the mean of offensive and defensive rating, rounded down to the nearest value). Within each band, monsters are presented alphabetically by name. Each entry has the individual ratings as well; it also indicates whether the creature is legendary.
