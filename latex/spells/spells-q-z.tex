\begin{DndTable}{XX}

1-45 & \nameref{lineage:human} \\
46-55 & \nameref{lineage:dwarf} \\
56-65 & \nameref{lineage:ihmisi} \\
66-75 & \nameref{lineage:orc} \\
76-85 & \nameref{lineage:gwerin} \\
86-90 & \nameref{lineage:halfling} \\
91-95 & \nameref{lineage:dragonborn} \\
96-100 & \nameref{lineage:jazuu}
\end{DndTable}

The reincarnated creature recalls its former life and experiences. It retains the capabilities it had in its original form, except it exchanges its original lineage for the new one and changes its lineage traits accordingly. Its culture does not change.
