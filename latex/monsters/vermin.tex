\section{Appendix: Vermin}\label{section:vermin}

\DndMonsterAction{Hold Breath} While out of water, the octopus can hold its breath for 30 minutes.

\DndMonsterAction{Underwater Camouflage} The octopus has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made while underwater.

\DndMonsterAction{Water Breathing} The octopus can breathe only underwater.

\DndMonsterSection{Actions}
\DndMonsterAttack[
	name=Tentacles,
	distance=melee,
	type=weapon,
	mod=+2,
	dmg=1,
	dmg-type=bludgeoning,
	extra={, and the target is grappled (escape DC 10). Until this grapple ends, the octopus can't use its tentacles on another target.}
]

\DndMonsterAction{Ink Cloud}
A 5-foot-radius cloud of ink extends all around the octopus if it is underwater. The area is heavily obscured for 1 minute, although a significant current can disperse the ink. After releasing the ink, the octopus can use the Dash action as a bonus action.
\end{DndMonster}
